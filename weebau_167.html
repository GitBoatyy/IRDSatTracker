Mission details:

  • SpaceX carried out its 6th Iridium-Next deployment mission on Jan 01, 2019.
    Ten satellites were deployed into initial orbits which will later be raised by the satellites′ own propulsion systems.
  • Deployment of the Iridium Next constellation was completed on Jan 11, 2019 when a Falcon 9 launch from Vandenberg placed the final 10 satellites in orbit. The Falcon 9 first stage, B1041, landed successfully on the JRTI droneship. The second stage was deorbited near Antarctica.
